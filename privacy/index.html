Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Veera. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect includes:

  • Account Data: We collect personal data when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Fitness Data: We collect data you provide about your fitness, including but not to, weight, body fat percentage, workout logs, and nutrition information.
  • Uploaded Images/Reports: We may collect images or reports you upload to the app for analysis and tracking.
  • Analytics: We use third-party analytics services to help us understand your usage of the services and to improve the services.
  • Authentication: We use authentication services to verify your identity.
  • Optional Health Integrations: With your consent, we may access data from health integration services (e.g., Google Fit, Apple HealthKit).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our services.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners.

4. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your data at any time. Please see our Data Deletion page for more information.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
